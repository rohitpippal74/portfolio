My Journey

Hello, my name is Rohit Pippal. I am a passionate Full Stack Web Developer with more than 5 years of experience in building scalable, secure, and performance-driven web applications. My journey in the tech world started early with my academic background. I completed my 10th and 12th in the science stream, where I built my foundation in logical thinking, mathematics, and computer basics. Later, I pursued my Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA) from Dr. Bhimrao Ambedkar University, Agra, which gave me a strong grounding in programming, databases, and software development.

After graduation, I started my professional journey at Amsoft, where I worked on enterprise-level PHP and MySQL projects. Later, I joined MS Digital Marketing Agency, where I developed marketing solutions and handled full-stack projects. Since April 2022, I have been working as aSenior Developer at Digiinteracts, where I manage end-to-end application development, mentor juniors, and work on high-performance platforms.

My technical expertise includes PHP (5+ years), Laravel (4+ years), JavaScript (4+ years), MySQL, REST APIs, and integration of modern AI models such as ChatGPT and Gemini. I follow best practices like the Laravel MVC architecture, implement Laravel Breeze for authentication, and Laravel Sanctum for API security. On the frontend, I have solid command over CSS, Bootstrap, jQuery, and I am continuously learning React & Next.jsto build even more dynamic and modern web applications.

Over my career, I have delivered 15+ projects across industries like Education, Organic Products, Marketing, E-learning, AI-based platforms, and Service Marketplaces. Some of the highlighted projects include:
